About Me








Hello! My name is Chloe Hill and I was born and raised in Red Lodge, Montana. In 2019, I moved to Missoula, Montana to attend the University of Montana. I graduated in 2023 with a Bachelor of Arts and a minor in Business Administration. I am also a graduate of the Davidson Honors College at UM.

While studying at the University of Montana, I was able to explore several aspects of Media Arts, including Animation, Digital Design, Sound Design, and Web Design. I am proficient in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Reaper. When coding, I use Visual Studio Code and work with HTML and CSS.
