About Me








Hello! My name is Chloe Hill and I'm from Red Lodge, Montana. This is where I grew up before moving to Missoula, Montana to attend the University of Montana. I graduated here December 2023 with a bachelor of arts and a minor in Business Administration while being a part of the Davidson Honors College.

During my time at the University of Montana, I was able to explore several aspects of Media Arts. Most notable of these are Animation, Digital Design, Sound Design, and Web Design. With this, I am proficient in Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Reaper. For coding, I mostly use Visual Studio Code and work with HTML and CSS.
